**Current stage:** active research & development  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-04

This project is currently being rebuilt from scratch after identifying conceptual issues in the initial prototype.

The goal is to design a neural-network-driven Adaptive Optical Modulation pipeline that correctly reflects the physical behavior of Free-Space Optics (FSO) channels.

---

## What's done

- [x] Initial prototype combining neural networks with adaptive optical modulation
- [x] Early experiments with mixed regression and classification
- [x] Identification of architectural and conceptual issues
- [x] Reformulation of the problem into physically meaningful stages

---

## In progress
